Are you a

Mobile Web Developer?

Are you sick of looking up your Mac's IP and typing it into your phone's browser?
  1. curl -O http://lilup.co/lil && chmod +x lil && ./lil my-app 8080
  2. http://my-app.lilup.co will then redirect to your local dev server.
  3. When your IP address changes, run lil <name> [<port>] again.

No more typing IPs.


And holler if you know of a simple, cross-platform way to watch network interface addresses (without polling).
